About Me

Here is more information about me

Water Sky

You might wonder why my name is "Water Sky". This is because my Chinese name literally means "Water" and "Sky" from direct translation.I have lived in 4 different places before. I was born in China. At the age of 14, I went to Wisconsin by myself and lived there for two years. In my junior year, I moved to Southern California and finished the rest of my high school. And now, I lived in Seattle to continue my education. I don't know where my next stop will be, but I would always carry the same passion and curiosity I have wherever I go.

Being a student at the University of Washington, I learn how to embrace diversity and overcome challenges. UW provided me with a great learning environment as well as numerous resources and kind professors. I would love to see more and greater students become a huskie and explore this amazing campus!
